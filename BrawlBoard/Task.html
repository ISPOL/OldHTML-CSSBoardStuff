The Rope Trap


"AHHHHH! Let me down! Let me down!" shrieked the shining tentacle. His frantic movements made the cave passage seem to swoon, illuminated by the world's first swinging living lightbulb.
"Squirt your ink! It'll make you more slippery and smaller and you'll fall out!" ordered the wizard distractedly, scribbling at a piece of parchment. "And stop swinging so much!"
    Wizard's notes:
  • Obstacle Name: Rope Trap
  • Danger Level: Low
  • Method of Remedy: Ink Squirts
  • Side Effects: Dehydration
  • Path to Dragon: Negative
  • Requires Explosives: Negative
  • Addendum: Subject Krzysiekxd seems quite displeased with this treatment. This is only the first trap. Must investigate further motivating actions


The Boulder Trap


"AHHHHH!" shrieked the white tentacle. Why was he screaming already wondered Aves. Poking his sucker into the hallway, the wizard caught sight of a large rolling stone, and quickly jerked the appendage back. As the boulder rolled out of the entrance, Aves quickly moved towards the flattened body of the bard. Scooting closer to the diminishing light, he hurriedly took some notes before the illumination ran out. Drat. He'd have to actually do something this time.
    Wizard's notes:
  • Obstacle Name: Boulder Trap
  • Danger Level: High
  • Method of Remedy: Rewind Time
  • Side Effects: Confusion
  • Path to Dragon: Negative
  • Requires Explosives: Negative
  • Addendum: The first death. Luckily he doesn't remember it. Will have to be more careful. Less distractions!

The Cage


"AHHHHH!" screamed the glowing tentacle. "Get me out!"
What now wondered Aves. Poking his sucker into the hallway, the wizard caught sight of the bard's predicament. "Stop screaming, grab your cutlass, and just cut yourself out. It's made out of this weak land material called 'wood.'" Shaking his top sucker, the wizard jotted a few more notes onto the map.

Rubble


"AHHH-" the bard was muffled with a quick slap.
"Quiet!" The wizard pointed to the cracks in the rubble. "There's the dragon, sleeping! We've accomplished our goal. Just set the explosives into the Pulverizer and we can get out of here!"
"That's all the traps?" asked the bard.
"Yes. A job well done!"
"Phew, I'm glad I didn't die springing all those traps!" the bard exclaimed.
"Yeah, how about that..." the wizard replied nervously.
    Wizard's notes:
  • Obstacle Name: Rubble
  • Danger Level: Low
  • Method of Remedy: Pulverizer, Explosives
  • Side Effects: Opens up the passage
  • Path to Dragon: Confirmed
  • Requires Explosives: Confirmed
